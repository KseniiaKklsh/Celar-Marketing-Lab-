The starting point

A brand with strong values and three markets to win

Vigor & Sage produces premium herbal pet food scientifically formulated with functional plants — ginseng, turmeric, lion's mane. The product is exceptional. But in three separate EU markets, the brand needed to speak three languages and to three different audiences without losing its identity.

Build brand recognition in NL, FR, and broader EU markets simultaneously
Educate audiences on why herbs matter in pet nutrition — a non-obvious message
Maintain consistent visual identity and brand voice across all three accounts
Increase audience engagement and community interaction beyond passive scrolling
NL · FR accounts — same brand, local voice
Our approach

System-first. Science-backed. Community-driven.

We built a unified content architecture that could be localised — not just translated. Each market received a tailored content plan with shared brand pillars: education, visual consistency, and emotional connection with pet owners.

Educational Content Series
Herb Spotlight series — deep dives into ginseng, lion's mane, turmeric. Science for pet owners, not scientists.
Community Activation
Online & offline challenges, giveaways, and live audience interviews — turning followers into advocates.
Visual Brand System
Photo days, influencer collaborations, and strict visual templates — every post unmistakably Vigor & Sage.
Multilingual Localisation
Dutch, French, and English content — each adapted to cultural context, not just translated word for word.
Content that educates and converts
Three accounts, one system

Local presence at European scale

Each Instagram account serves a distinct market and audience profile, but operates within a shared content framework. The brand voice stays consistent; the message adapts to what matters locally.

Ax Lindholm is a founder and investor operating at the intersection of venture capital and operational building. He had deep insights worth sharing — on VC models, startups, European tech. But his LinkedIn presence wasn't translating that depth into visibility or leads.

Build recognition as a thought leader in the Dutch and European startup/VC ecosystem
Turn LinkedIn into an active lead generation channel for business products
Create consistent, high-quality content that reflects his actual expertise — not generic "founder tips"
Reach the right audience: senior professionals, founders, investors in the EU market

Our approach

Content that sounds like him — and reaches people who matter

We built a personal brand content system anchored in Ax's actual perspective on venture capital, startups, and the European market. No ghostwriting fluff — sharp takes, data-backed posts, stories that resonate with founders and senior decision-makers.

Thought Leadership Content
Original posts on VC trends, startup dynamics, and EU market insights. Formats that work on LinkedIn: carousels, long-form, provocative takes.
Audience Targeting
Content engineered to reach founders, senior professionals, and investors — not vanity metrics. 28% senior-level audience, 13% founders.
Viral Post Architecture
