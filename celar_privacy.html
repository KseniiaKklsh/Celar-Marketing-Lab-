Who we are

Celar Marketing Lab ("CML", "we", "us") is a strategic marketing agency based in the Netherlands. We provide marketing strategy, content, and brand development services for EU businesses.

  • Business name: Celar Marketing Lab
  • Country: Netherlands (EU)
  • Email: kseniia@celarlab.com

What data we collect

We collect data only when you actively provide it or when it is necessary to deliver our services:

  • Contact form: your name, email address, and message content
  • Quiz: your email address (optional) and anonymous quiz answers
  • Product purchases: email address processed via Stripe (we do not store payment card data)
  • Waitlist signups: email address only
  • Cookies: we use minimal functional cookies and, with your consent, analytics cookies

How we use your data

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide our services
  • To send you information you explicitly requested
  • To process payments for digital products (via Stripe)
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • We do not use your data for automated decision-making
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

Legal basis (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for newsletter/waitlist signups and analytics cookies
  • Contract — when processing is necessary to fulfil a purchase or service agreement
  • Legitimate interest — for responding to inquiries and improving our services

Third-party services

We use the following trusted third-party services that may process your data:

  • Stripe — payment processing (governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Web3Forms — contact form submissions
  • Vercel — website hosting (EU-compatible infrastructure)
  • Google Fonts — typography (loaded from Google servers)
  • Calendly — booking calls (governed by Calendly's Privacy Policy)

All third-party processors are GDPR-compliant or operate under standard contractual clauses.

Cookies

We use cookies to make our website work properly and, with your consent, to understand how it is used.

  • Functional cookies: necessary for the site to work (e.g. remembering your cookie consent). Always active.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand visitor behaviour. Only set with your consent.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

How long we keep your data

  • Contact form submissions: up to 2 years
  • Purchase records: 7 years (legal requirement in the Netherlands)
  • Email list signups: until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Analytics data: anonymised, retained for up to 13 months

Your rights under GDPR

As an EU resident you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at kseniia@celarlab.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption, limited access controls, and use of reputable third-party processors. No system is 100% secure — if you have concerns, contact us directly.

Children's privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please contact us immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
